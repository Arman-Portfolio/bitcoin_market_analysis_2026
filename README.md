# Bitcoin Market Analysis (2026)

## Overview
This project analyzes Bitcoin price movements from January 2026 to April, focusing on identifying crash periods, volume-driven trends, and market recovery behavior.

The goal is to understand how price and volume interact to signal panic selling, accumulation, and trend strength.

---

## Dataset
- Period: Jan 2026 – Present
- Fields: Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Volume

---

## Key Metrics & Calculations

### Daily Return (%)
Measures day-to-day price change.

### Volatility
Difference between High and Low price.

### Moving Average (7-day)
Used to identify trend direction.

---

## Crash Detection 

| Type       | Meaning             |
|------------|--------------------|
| REAL CRASH | High-volume selling (institutional exit) |
| FAKE DIP   | Temporary decline  |
| NORMAL     | Stable movement    |

---

---

## Key Insights

- Significant crash observed in late January to early February (~35% drop)
- High trading volume confirmed panic selling behavior
- Strong support identified around 60K–65K
- Resistance observed near 73K–75K
- Market transitioned through three phases:
  - Panic (high-volume decline)
  - Stabilization (sideways movement)
  - Recovery (gradual uptrend)
